Course Details - TOEFL

TOEFL-Objectives

est of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) is a standardized test to assess the English language proficiency of non-native speakers willing to enroll in colleges/universities in English-speaking countries. It is accepted by more than 11000 reputed universities in more than 150 countries. It is managed and conducted by the Education Testing System (ETS). There are two types of TOEFL exams; TOEFL iBT which is computer-based, and the other one is TOEFL paper-based examination. TOEFL is mostly attempted by students who are planning to pursue higher education abroad.
TOEFL tests the English language proficiency of non-native English speakers based on four sections- Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing. The four sections of TOEFL are designed to assess the overall English language skills and how proficient the applicants are while communicating in English. It is important to check their communication skills before they settle down in an English-speaking country so that they can adjust themselves to Universities and colleges where the mode of instruction is in the English language.


TOEFL-Eligibility

There are no official eligibility criteria as set by the conducting body ETS ( Education Testing System) for the TOEFL exam. The examination is intended for non-native English speakers to take up work or study abroad programs in an English-speaking country. There is no such official eligibility or prerequisite set by the conducting body for the test takers. Both the TOEFL iBT and TOEFL paper-based examination is an open examination to prove English language proficiency and skills before taking admission or applying for the immigration process. The TOEFL examination can be taken by any student or skilled worker from a non-native English-speaking environment. There is no official restriction regarding the minimum or maximum age limit for students to appear for the test. The TOEFL test is designed as per the university level and it is recommended that the test is taken by students who have completed a minimum Class 10 or high school (Class 12) from a recognized board. In terms of academic qualification, there are no minimum and maximum academic qualification requirements for the test takers. There are no e ligibility criteria regarding the academic scores or degree.


TOEFL Eligibility: Age Limit

Furthermore, the ETS, the authority that administers this exam, has set no age limit. This exam is divided into four sections: reading, writing, listening and speaking, according to the TOEFL test pattern. Overall, the candidates' performance is given a score of 120.


TOEFL Eligibility: Exam Qualification

TOEFL examination registration can be done through the official website, phone, and email ID. If you want to book your test slot by phone, you need to look for the contact number of the nearest TOEFL examination center. Over the call, you need to share every personal detail as stated in your identification proof. Make sure you offer them accurate details as it is there in the ID proof. You need to confirm the test date and time slot over the phone. Once you are done with the booking, you need to pay the registration fee. It has to be done through online mode, and you will soon receive a confirmation.
